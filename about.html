About Apprentricity

Down to study tools for electricity students at Atlantic Technical College.

If you're in school studying for a career as a professional electrician, you're probably filled with anxiety (*ahem* excitement). And if you're like me, then you're also juggling a personal life and a job...car troubles? Jeez.

With all of these things in your life, I understand it can be challenging to find the time to study and find the right information to help you during class time.

That's why I made Apprentricity.com. With this simple website I hope to help electricity students, present and future, nail their understanding of the foundational topics in this great trade.

Use the simple quizzes and informational resources here to reinforce your knowledge for class or as a refresher while studying for your journeyman's exam. This isn't meant to be the last resource you'll ever need, think of it more like another tool in your toolbelt.

To your continued success,

David, Electricity Student
ATC Coral Springs, FL. 2019
