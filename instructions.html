Instructions

You can use Apprentricity to study in 3 easy steps:

1. Choose Your Chapter

Quizzes on Apprentricity are organized as multiple choice selections taken directly from the Textbook. Pick the one you need most help with.

2. Take A Quiz

Every quiz is a made up of a series of up to six questions from each chapter. Take a quiz muliple times to help yourself retain the information. The quizzes will never contain trick questions.

3. See Your Results

Compare your answers to the test bank right away to see how well you did. If you feel like you got it, great! And if you feel like you could use more practice, no problem. Just take another quiz.

Additional Instructions

To help you study even more effectively, Apprentricity uses Quizlet for the quiz-taking interface.

Apprentricity Resources

Along with the simple quizzes, you can find a selection of helpful resources to assist you with your studies. The Resources page features a list of references to the National Electrical Code (NEC), links to useful websites, and videos you to get you excited about the trade.

Reporting Errors & Bugs

I try my best to keep this website simple and free of errors in code and material. However, I cannot guarantee this website will be without problems. In the event you find an error (e.g. grammar, misspellings, dead links, etc.) please let me know.


Send what you find to Apprentricity-Errors[at]grindfusion.com, and I'll handle it as soon as I can.
